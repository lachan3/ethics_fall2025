title: "Paying players"
layout: archive
---

These are the players that spend money on games. These range from players that like to spend a couple dollars to thousands of dollars. Some of these people are susceptible to incentivized purchases, such as people with little time available to play games, people with a lot of money, and players who aren’t as skilled at the game.
